# Symbolic Logic
##### General
- The idea of using syumbols to mechanize the process of deductive reasonning
	- similar to algebraic notation mechanizing process of reasonning with numbers
- Has provided the theoretical basis for many areas of computer science
	- digital logic circuit design, ai
- Logic **does not** help determine the truth/falsity of the component statements, rather it links the seperate pieces of info into a coherent whole
