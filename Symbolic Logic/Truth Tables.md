### Conjunction ($\land$)

### Disjunction ($\lor$)
##### Inclusive or
The statement pvq is true when p **and/or** q are true
![](images/Pasted image 20220912172432.png)


##### Exclusive or ($p \lor q\land \sim (p \land q)$)
